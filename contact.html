In the world of Naruto, contacting Sasuke Uchiha is no simple task, as he often operates in isolation and moves discreetly. Typically, the most reliable way to contact him is through Naruto Uzumaki, his closest ally and the Hokage, who maintains occasional communication with him. Sasuke also keeps in touch with his family, particularly his wife, Sakura, and daughter, Sarada. In some situations, a messenger hawk—a traditional ninja method for long-distance communication—could reach him if his location is known. However, given his penchant for secrecy and constant travels, direct contact requires a good understanding of his movements and purpose.
